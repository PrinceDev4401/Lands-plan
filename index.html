Why Prampram?

Prampram boasts of a serene and tranquil environment, largely free from noise and air pollution common in urban areas.

This peaceful setting, coupled with its stunning coastline featuring several pristine beaches, makes it an attractive destination for both tourists and those seeking a healthier and more peaceful atmosphere.

Furthermore, Prampram's proximity to Tema and numerous free zone companies offers significant economic advantages.

This location presents excellent opportunities for investors seeking a high return on their investments.

Road expansion of Tema–Prampram road into a 6-lane road has begun and may be completed within a year or two.

Driving from Tema to Prampram could be less than 20 minutes.
